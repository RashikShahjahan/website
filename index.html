About me

Big architecture nerd. In love with C and C++. Immensely interested in the game engine architecture and graphics rendering pipeline. I have previously worked with Autodesk on their Stingray Game engine and a Deloitte innovation lab where we built a real-time communication architecture for large-scale sensor networks from the ground up. From my experience there, I have come to appreciate architecturally sound and well optimized software. I also carry a great interest towards triple-A game engine pipelines.

Outside of work, I like spending my time reading to learn more about things that interest me. I also enjoy writing and using open source software: big fan of Linux (Arch user here) and FOSS. Outside of programming, I'm a computer hardware enthusiast, PC gamer, occassional couch potato, and a little into atheletics. On a pursuit towards a safer world.
